Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about Web Dev Conf 2026? Here are some of the most common questions attendees ask before joining the event.

Who can attend Web Dev Conf 2026?

Anyone interested in web development is welcome! Whether you're a beginner, student, freelancer, or experienced developer, the conference offers valuable sessions for every skill level.

When and where will the conference take place?

Web Dev Conf 2026 will be held in April 2026. The venue and online participation details will be shared with registered attendees before the event.

Will I receive a certificate after attending?

Yes. Participants who complete the conference registration and attend the event will receive a digital certificate of participation.

What topics will be covered?

The conference includes sessions on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js, Node.js, AI for web development, cybersecurity, UI/UX design, performance optimization, and career development.
